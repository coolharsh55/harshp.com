Terms of (Ab)Use: An Analysis of GenAI Services

2026-06-25T11:00:00 Conference
Conference on Fairness, Accountability, and Transparency (FAccT)
✍ Harshvardhan J. Pandit* , Dick A. H. Blankvoort , Adel Shaaban , Sasha Luccioni , Abeba Birhane
Description: We analyse the terms of 6 generative AI services from the perspective of an EU-based consumer and reiterate known issues such as use for training and surface new concerns regarding responsibility, liability, and rights. We make concrete recommendations to urgently upgrade existing consumer protection mechanisms.
